FAQ

Common Questions

Will my smart contracts be audited?
Internal review and full test coverage are standard on every project. For mainnet launches handling real value, we coordinate with third-party auditors (CertiK, Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin) - cost passed through, no markup.
Which chains do you build on?
Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, BNB Chain and Solana. We pick based on your users, gas profile and tooling needs, not hype.
Can you integrate with my existing dApp?
Yes - we work with deployed contracts, add new modules, build admin tools and frontends. We always start by reading existing code before touching anything.
Do you do tokenomics?
We collaborate on token design but always recommend pairing with a tokenomics specialist for vesting, distribution and game theory. We handle the implementation.
What happens after deployment?
Mainnet deploy, contract verification, frontend launch and monitoring. 30 days of post-launch support included. Most clients keep us on retainer for upgrades.
