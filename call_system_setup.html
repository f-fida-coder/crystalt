Call System Setup

Service Details

We build and configure call infrastructure that just works - VoIP, Anveo, FreePBX, Asterisk, Twilio, 3CX and the rest. Whether you're a 3-person team that needs reliable extensions or a 50-seat call centre running a predictive dialler, we set up the system, integrate it with your CRM, train your team and stay on call for the first month.

Every deployment is engineered for the realities of telephony: dropped calls, codec mismatches, NAT traversal, carrier quirks, and regional compliance. We test against real phone numbers in real conditions before handing the keys over.

Most teams come to us frustrated with vague pricing, lock-in contracts and "managed services" that nobody actually manages. We give you a fixed quote, the credentials, and clean documentation. You run the system - we're around when you need us.

Custom Call Routing

Route by time, queue, language, skill or load. Ring groups, round-robin, simultaneous or hunt - whatever fits your workflow.

Advanced Call Forwarding

Forward to mobile, alternate lines, follow-the-sun teams or external answering services with full caller ID preservation.

IVR & Auto-attendant

Multi-level menus, voice-to-email, voicemail transcription, after-hours messaging and language selection trees.

Predictive Diallers

Outbound campaigns at scale with answer-machine detection, do-not-call list enforcement and live agent connection rates over 30%.

CRM Integration

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho or custom. Click-to-call, screen pops, automatic logging, post-call surveys.

Recording & Compliance

Encrypted call recording, configurable retention, opt-out flows, GDPR / HIPAA-aware deployments and tamper-proof archives.

Real-Time Dashboards

Live agent status, queue depth, abandonment rate, average wait time and SLA tracking - on screen for supervisors.

AI Transcription

Optional Whisper-powered call transcription with sentiment scoring, keyword alerts and searchable archives.

Security & Fraud Protection

TLS+SRTP encryption, geo-blocking, rate limits and fraud detection to stop toll fraud before it hits your invoice.

Training & 30-Day Support

Admin and agent training, written documentation, and direct access to our engineers for the first 30 days post-launch.

FAQ

Common Questions

Which platforms do you set up?
Anveo, FreePBX, Asterisk, Twilio, 3CX, Genesys. We pick based on your call volume, integrations and budget. Free advice on which one fits before you commit.
Can you migrate from my current provider?
Yes - we port numbers, recreate IVR flows, migrate recordings and run both systems in parallel until you're confident, then cut over with zero downtime.
Do you do CRM integration?
Yes - HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, custom. Click-to-call, screen pops, automatic call logging, post-call surveys.
What about call recording and compliance?
Encrypted recording, configurable retention, opt-out flows for jurisdictions that need them. GDPR / HIPAA-aware deployments available.
Will you train my team?
Yes - admin walkthroughs, agent training, documentation and a 30-day support window included. Most teams are self-sufficient within a week.
