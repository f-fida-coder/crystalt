FAQ

Common Questions

Will my trading bot be profitable?
We don't sell strategies - we build the bot to execute your strategy reliably. Profitability depends on your edge. We do build backtesting frameworks so you can validate strategies before going live.
Can you integrate with Telegram / Discord / WhatsApp?
Yes - all major chat platforms plus Slack, Microsoft Teams and custom webhooks. We handle authentication, rate limits and platform-specific quirks.
Do you use AI / LLMs?
Yes when it fits - OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, open-source models on our own GPUs. Prompt engineering, RAG, function-calling, agent frameworks like LangGraph.
Is hosting included?
First 30 days yes. After that you can host on our infra ($20-200/mo depending on load) or take the Docker container and run it yourself - your call.
How do I monitor a bot once it's live?
Every bot ships with a dashboard or alerting setup (Telegram alerts, Slack notifications, or a real-time web UI). You know when it stops, slows down or hits an error.
