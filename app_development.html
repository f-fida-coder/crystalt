FAQ

Common Questions

How long does an MVP usually take?
A focused single-platform MVP ships in 2-3 weeks. Cross-platform apps with backend, payments and analytics typically take 4-8 weeks. We commit to dates in writing after the discovery call.
Do you build in React Native or native?
Both. React Native is our default - one codebase, lower cost, fast iteration. We go native (Swift / Kotlin) when you need ARKit, advanced camera, low-level hardware or absolute performance.
Do you handle App Store and Play Store submission?
Yes. We submit on your developer accounts, write metadata, generate screenshots and handle rejection responses. Your accounts, your apps - we just drive the upload.
Who owns the code?
You do, from commit one. We push to your GitHub / GitLab and hand over all credentials at launch. No vendor lock-in.
What about post-launch bugs?
30 days of bug fixes and monitoring included. After that we offer optional retainer plans for ongoing work.
