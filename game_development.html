FAQ

Common Questions

Unity or Unreal?
Unity for mobile, 2D and most cross-platform games. Unreal Engine 5 for high-fidelity 3D, console-grade visuals and competitive shooters. We help you pick during discovery.
Do you do art too?
We have in-house 2D and 3D artists for casual and mid-core games. For AAA-level art direction, we partner with specialist studios under one project umbrella.
Can you publish to Steam / consoles?
Yes for Steam (we handle Steamworks, achievements, build pipelines). For consoles (PS / Xbox / Switch), you need a registered developer account - we build the certified build and walk you through submission.
How do you handle monetization?
Ads, IAP, season passes, paid up-front - we build whichever fits your audience. We never recommend predatory designs and won't ship pay-to-win without consent.
What about updates and live ops?
Optional retainer plans for seasons, events and content drops. Many of our games launched with us still get monthly updates two years later.
